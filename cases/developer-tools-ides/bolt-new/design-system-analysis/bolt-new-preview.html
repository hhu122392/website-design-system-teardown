05 - Responsive Behavior

Mobile keeps the prompt, collapses grids, and avoids overflow

At 390px, nav links collapse, the prompt stays central, brand cards become a horizontal rail, and pricing cards stack.

Observed mobile rules

  • Home headline wraps while preserving the 48px display feel.
  • Prompt composer remains the primary first-screen component.
  • Pricing cards stack into one column.
  • Design-system cards can scroll horizontally on small screens.
  • No horizontal page overflow was observed in the checked mobile view.

06 - Known Gaps

Scope and evidence limits

This preview uses public Bolt assets and public-page observations. It is not an official Bolt guideline.

  • Logged-in Builder, workspace, and project editor surfaces were not inspected.
  • The Resources dropdown did not successfully open during browser inspection, so dropdown contents are not represented as verified.
  • No prompt, email form, sign-in, purchase, upgrade, or account-changing action was submitted.
  • Remote source images/icons are loaded from public Bolt URLs to satisfy the source-icon requirement.
